Carrot Cake

Ingredients

To prepare this recipe for a carrot cake, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup of oil
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 1⁄2 cups of flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup of chopped pineapple in syrup
  • 1⁄2 cup of finely chopped pecans
  • 2 cups of grated carrot

Steps

The procedure is simple, actually. My mother-in-law taught me the dough/mixture could even be done in a blender. Follow along the next steps.

  1. Pour the "dry" ingredients (i.e. sugar, flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt) in one big bowl and mix them well (preferably use a whisk).
  2. In another bowl, mix the oil, with the eggs. For the eggs, pour one by one in another small bowl previously to avoid mixing spoiled ones into the big mixture.
  3. Blend all of the ingredients mentioned in the previous steps, get a homogeneous mixture.
  4. Cover the chopped pecans in flour, add them to the mixture. Add the carrot and the pineapple too.
  5. Put the mixture into the baking recipient previously covered with butter. Put in the oven at 175 degrees Celsius and bake for 30 minutes
