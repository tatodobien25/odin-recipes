Huevos Rancheros

Description

Huevos rancheros is a mexican way of preparing eggs. It is mainly the following: eggs (typically fried, but some do them scrambled) put on a fried tortilla, that way the tortilla is cruncy. You put on some fried beans on top of the tortilla, then the fried eggs and finally, the sauce as a topping for it all.

Ingredients

For a personal portion, you will need the following:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tortillas
  • Fried Beans
  • Oil
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 medium-sized onion
  • 1 chile serrano
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Powdered chicken bouillion

Steps

For the preparation, wash your vegetables and grab a sharp knife! Then follow the steps listed below.

  1. Chop 2 of the tomatoes and 1 chile serrano finely. Put the other 2 tomatoes to boil.
  2. Fry the chopped tomatoes and chile with a little oil, season with chicken bouillion, salt and ground pepper.
  3. When the tomatoes and the chile are letting go of their juices, mash them in the same pot you are frying them. Turn off the heat of the other 2 tomatoes boiling.
  4. Blenderize the 2 boiled tomatoes, do not add any salt or water (you can substitute these with canned tomato puree).
  5. Add the blended tomatoes to the mashed tomatoes and chile.
  6. Rinse the blender container with the water used to boil the tomatoes, and add it to the sauce.
  7. Chop the white onion coarsely. Add it to the sauce. One more time season with salt, chicken bouillion and ground black pepper. Stir it a little.
  8. On a separate skillet, fry 2 tortillas and get your beans ready.
  9. Put on some beans on the fried tortillas and spread them well. Cook your eggs (fried or scrambled).
  10. Place your cooked eggs on top of the fried tortillas.
  11. Serve the sauce on top of the eggs and tortillas, make sure everything is covered by the sauce.
  12. Enjoy your meal!
