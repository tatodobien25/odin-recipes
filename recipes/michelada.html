Michelada

The following is a way to have a refreshing cocktail based on beer!

Ingredients

To prepare this delicious cocktail, you will need the following ingredients

  • Ice
  • Beer
  • Lime juice
  • Salt
  • Tequila
  • Clamato juice

Steps

This is a very easy cocktail to prepare, but anyway, here's the order the author prefers to add the ingredients.

  1. On a big glass, put a lot of ice. Add the juice of half a lime and sprinkle some salt.
  2. Add tequila (optional), clamato, and fill the rest with the beer of your preference.
  3. Enjoy with moderation.
